TerraSourcing

TerraSourcing is a B2B platform designed to connect importers and exporters, creating a streamlined marketplace for businesses to find and trade products globally. The platform facilitates interactions between suppliers and buyers by providing a user-friendly interface where they can register, list products, and communicate directly.

The project was initially developed in WordPress and moved to the React.

Overview

The primary objective of TerraSourcing is to create a seamless and efficient B2B marketplace that connects importers and exporters across the globe. The platform aims to: Facilitate trade by simplifying the process of finding reliable business partners. Provide businesses with a space to showcase their products and services in an organized manner. Enable companies to expand their reach globally by offering intuitive search, filter, and communication tools. Streamline the import/export process by offering essential resources, such as product listings, buyer-seller communication, and up-to-date industry information.

  • Facilitate Global Trade Connections: Enable businesses to connect with reliable importers and exporters worldwide, fostering relationships that lead to successful trade partnerships and transactions.
  • Enhance Product Visibility: Provide a platform for exporters to showcase their products effectively, utilizing features like detailed listings and search optimization to increase visibility and attract potential buyers.
  • Streamline Communication and Transactions: Implement efficient communication tools that allow seamless interaction between buyers and sellers, simplifying negotiations and transaction processes to reduce time and effort in closing deals.

Key Features

Feature 1

Registration & User Management: Businesses can sign up and manage their profiles to showcase their offerings.

Feature 2

Product Listing: Exporters can list their products with descriptions, images, and categories, making it easy for importers to find relevant products.

Feature 3

Search & Filters: Importers can search for products or suppliers based on various criteria such as category, location, or price.

Feature 4

Communication Tools: TerraSourcing provides direct messaging between importers and exporters to negotiate deals and discuss details.

AgriTech is an innovative farming application designed to help students and farmers learn modern and traditional farming techniques. User-Friendly Interface

Different Farming Techniques

Crop Selection Assistance

– Suggests the best crops based on soil type and climate.
